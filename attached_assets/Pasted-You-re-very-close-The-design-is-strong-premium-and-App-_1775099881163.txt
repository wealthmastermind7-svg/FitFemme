🔥 Fix Your Monetisation (High Conversion Version)

🧠 Core Principle

Users don’t pay for more usage
They pay for:
	•	Better results
	•	Better experience
	•	Identity (“Pro version of me”)

⸻

✅ RECOMMENDED PAYWALL STRUCTURE

🆓 FREE PLAN (Keep them hooked)

Make free useful but LIMITED in outcomes:

Free users get:
	•	1–2 workouts per day (not 3, reduce this)
	•	Access to basic workouts only (e.g. 5–8 total)
	•	No customization
	•	No streak tracking beyond today
	•	Limited workout categories (lock HIIT / advanced)

👉 Goal: They can start, but cannot progress properly

⸻

💎 PRO PLAN (Make it irresistible)

Pro users get:
	•	Unlimited workouts
	•	Full workout library (all categories unlocked)
	•	Workout programs (e.g. “7-Day Glow Up”, “Booty Builder”)
	•	Progress tracking (history + stats screen you already built)
	•	Streak system + calendar
	•	Custom workout builder
	•	No ads (even if you don’t have ads yet, say it)

⸻
