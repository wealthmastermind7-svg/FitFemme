Fit Femme provides general fitness and wellness information only and is not intended as medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The exercises, training plans, and fitness guidance presented in this app are for educational purposes only.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional, physician, or certified fitness trainer before:

  • Starting any new fitness program or exercise routine
